A little something about us

No, we don't make sandwiches ... maybe not unless you ask nicely, and that's exactly what we are.


Boolean Sandwiches is a tech company with it's eye currently on web development.


Our minimalistic approach allows us to focus on what needs to be done, trimming unnecessary bloat to produce stunning, responsive websites that perform well and look good on any device.


We can be a little weird sometimes, which is what makes us so great at what we do. Oh, and feel free to call us Bit Sammies, we know we do.
